About Indian Tourism

India is a land of diverse cultures, traditions, and landscapes. From the snow-capped Himalayas in the north to the tropical beaches in the south, India offers something for every traveler.

With UNESCO World Heritage Sites, ancient temples, modern cities, and wildlife sanctuaries, India is one of the most visited countries in the world.

Popular Tourist Places

Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal, Agra

Location: Uttar Pradesh

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, this white marble monument is a symbol of love built by Emperor Shah Jahan.

Best Time: October to March

Jaipur

Jaipur – Pink City

Location: Rajasthan

Known for its pink-colored buildings, magnificent forts, and royal palaces. Visit Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort.

Best Time: November to February

Kerala

Kerala Backwaters

Location: Kerala

Experience serene boat rides through palm-lined waterways. Known as "God's Own Country" for its natural beauty.

Best Time: September to March

Goa

Goa Beaches

Location: Goa

Famous for beautiful beaches, Portuguese architecture, and vibrant nightlife. A perfect tropical getaway.

Best Time: November to February

Ladakh

Ladakh

Location: Jammu & Kashmir

High-altitude desert with stunning monasteries, crystal-clear lakes, and adventure activities.

Best Time: May to September

Varanasi

Varanasi

Location: Uttar Pradesh

One of the oldest living cities in the world. Famous for its ghats along the holy river Ganges.

Best Time: October to March
